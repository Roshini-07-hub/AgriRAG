TERMITE CONTROL IN CROPS
========================

1. INTRODUCTION
Termites (Order: Isoptera) are social insects that cause significant damage to crops, especially in dryland areas. Major pest species: Odontotermes obesus, Microtermes obesi, Trinervitermes biformis. They cause yield losses of 10–30% in sugarcane, groundnut, and wheat.

2. IDENTIFICATION
- Workers: Pale, soft-bodied, wingless, 3–5 mm
- Soldiers: Larger head with mandibles
- Reproductives (alates): Winged, dark brown
- Queen: Highly enlarged abdomen, lays thousands of eggs daily

3. DAMAGE SYMPTOMS
- Hollowing of stems and roots
- Wilting and death of plants
- Mud tubes on plant stems and soil surface
- Soil-filled galleries in roots and tubers
- Sugarcane: Hollow stems, yellowing
- Groundnut: Damaged pods and roots
- Wheat: Dead hearts, root damage

4. FAVORABLE CONDITIONS
- Sandy, light soils
- Dry weather and drought conditions
- Presence of dead wood and organic matter
- Monoculture farming
- Reduced tillage

5. LIFE CYCLE
- Colony consists of queen, king, workers, soldiers, alates
- Queen lives 15–25 years
- Colony size: 1–3 million individuals
- Alates swarm during monsoon for new colony formation

6. MANAGEMENT STRATEGIES

6.1 Cultural Control
- Deep summer ploughing to expose and destroy colonies
- Remove tree stumps and dead wood from fields
- Avoid using undecomposed organic matter
- Crop rotation
- Flood irrigation in termite-prone areas

6.2 Biological Control
- Beauveria bassiana: Entomopathogenic fungus
- Metarhizium anisopliae: Entomopathogenic fungus
- Apply as soil drench around affected plants

6.3 Chemical Control
- Seed treatment:
  * Chlorpyrifos 20 EC at 12 mL/kg seed
  * Imidacloprid 70 WS at 5 g/kg seed
- Soil treatment at sowing:
  * Chlorpyrifos 20 EC at 4 L/ha in 500 L water
  * Fipronil 0.3% GR at 25 kg/ha
- Stem treatment:
  * Chlorpyrifos 20 EC at 2.5 mL/L drench around stem

6.4 Bait Stations
- Hexaflumuron or chlorfluazuron baits
- Place near termite mounds and mud tubes
- Effective for colony elimination

7. SUGARCANE SPECIFIC
- Treat setts with Chlorpyrifos 20 EC before planting
- Apply Fipronil 0.3% GR in furrows at planting
- Drench with Chlorpyrifos at first sign of damage
