WHEAT RUST DISEASE GUIDE
========================

1. INTRODUCTION
Wheat rust diseases are caused by Puccinia species and are among the most destructive diseases of wheat. Three types: Stem rust (P. graminis), Leaf rust (P. triticina), and Yellow/Stripe rust (P. striiformis).

2. TYPES AND SYMPTOMS

2.1 Stem Rust (Black Rust)
- Brick-red to dark brown pustules on stems and leaves
- Pustules turn black at maturity
- Severe lodging and stem breakage
- Caused by Puccinia graminis f. sp. tritici

2.2 Leaf Rust (Brown Rust)
- Orange-brown circular pustules on upper leaf surface
- Most common rust in India
- Causes 10–40% yield loss
- Caused by Puccinia triticina

2.3 Yellow Rust (Stripe Rust)
- Yellow-orange pustules in stripes on leaves
- Occurs in cool, moist conditions
- Serious in hills and northern plains
- Caused by Puccinia striiformis

3. FAVORABLE CONDITIONS
- Stem rust: Temperature 15–35°C, high humidity
- Leaf rust: Temperature 15–22°C, dew or rain
- Yellow rust: Temperature 2–15°C, high humidity

4. DISEASE CYCLE
- Uredospores spread by wind over long distances
- Alternate host: Barberry (Berberis vulgaris) for stem rust
- Overwinters as uredospores in southern India
- New races develop through mutation and hybridization

5. MANAGEMENT STRATEGIES

5.1 Resistant Varieties
- HD-2967, HD-3086 (resistant to leaf and yellow rust)
- PBW-343, PBW-550 (Punjab)
- K-9107, K-0307 (UP)
- Avoid susceptible varieties like Sonalika

5.2 Chemical Control
- Seed treatment: Carboxin 75 WP at 2.5 g/kg seed
- Foliar spray at first rust appearance:
  * Propiconazole 25 EC at 0.1% (1 mL/L)
  * Tebuconazole 25.9 EC at 1 mL/L
  * Mancozeb 75 WP at 2.5 g/L
- Repeat spray after 15 days if needed

5.3 Cultural Control
- Early sowing to avoid peak rust season
- Balanced fertilization (avoid excess N)
- Remove volunteer wheat plants
- Destroy infected crop debris

6. MONITORING
- Regular field scouting from tillering stage
- Use rust forecasting models
- Alert system through ICAR-IIWBR Karnal

7. ECONOMIC THRESHOLD
- Spray when 5% leaf area is affected
- Immediate action for stem rust appearance
