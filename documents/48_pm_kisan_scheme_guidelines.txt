PM-KISAN SCHEME GUIDELINES
===========================

1. INTRODUCTION
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) is a Central Sector Scheme launched on 24 February 2019. It provides income support of Rs. 6,000 per year to all landholding farmer families to supplement their financial needs for procuring inputs.

2. SCHEME OBJECTIVES
- Provide financial support to farmers for agricultural inputs
- Supplement income of small and marginal farmers
- Reduce distress among farming community
- Promote sustainable agriculture

3. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
- All landholding farmer families (husband, wife, and minor children)
- Cultivable land in their name as per land records
- Both small/marginal and large farmers are eligible
- Applicable to all states and UTs

4. EXCLUSION CRITERIA
- Institutional landholders
- Farmer families with one or more members who are/were:
  * Income tax payers
  * Constitutional post holders
  * Current/former Ministers, MPs, MLAs
  * Government employees (except Group D/Multi-Tasking Staff)
  * Professionals (doctors, engineers, lawyers, CAs)
  * Retired pensioners with monthly pension >Rs. 10,000

5. BENEFIT AMOUNT
- Rs. 6,000 per year in three equal installments
- Rs. 2,000 per installment
- Transferred directly to bank account (DBT)
- Installment periods: April–July, August–November, December–March

6. REGISTRATION PROCESS
- Visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC)
- Online registration at pmkisan.gov.in
- Documents required:
  * Aadhaar card
  * Land records (Khasra/Khatauni)
  * Bank account details
  * Mobile number

7. VERIFICATION PROCESS
- State/UT governments verify beneficiary data
- Aadhaar seeding mandatory for payment
- Land records verified by revenue officials

8. PAYMENT STATUS
- Check at pmkisan.gov.in
- SMS alerts on registered mobile number
- Helpline: 155261 or 1800115526 (toll-free)

9. SCHEME STATISTICS (as of 2023)
- Beneficiaries: 11+ crore farmers
- Total disbursement: Rs. 2.5+ lakh crore
- Coverage: All states and UTs

10. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
- PM-KISAN helpline: 155261
- Email: pmkisan-ict@gov.in
- State nodal officers
- District Agriculture Officers

11. RECENT UPDATES
- Face authentication for beneficiary verification
- eKYC mandatory for all beneficiaries
- Integration with Aadhaar for seamless payment
