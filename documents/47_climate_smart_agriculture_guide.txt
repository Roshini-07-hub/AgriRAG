CLIMATE-SMART AGRICULTURE GUIDE
================================

1. INTRODUCTION
Climate-Smart Agriculture (CSA) is an approach that helps transform agri-food systems toward green and climate-resilient practices. It aims to achieve three goals: sustainably increase productivity, adapt to climate change, and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

2. CLIMATE CHANGE IMPACTS ON AGRICULTURE
- Rising temperatures: Reduced crop yields, heat stress
- Erratic rainfall: Droughts and floods
- Sea level rise: Salinization of coastal farmlands
- Increased pest and disease pressure
- Reduced water availability

3. CSA PRACTICES

3.1 Improved Crop Varieties
- Heat-tolerant varieties: Wheat HD-3086, Rice Sahbhagi Dhan
- Flood-tolerant: Rice Swarna Sub-1 (survives 14 days submergence)
- Drought-tolerant: Millets, sorghum, drought-tolerant rice
- Salt-tolerant: CST-7-1 rice, Lunishree rice

3.2 Conservation Agriculture
- Minimum/zero tillage: Reduces fuel use and soil disturbance
- Permanent soil cover: Mulching, cover crops
- Crop rotation and diversification
- Happy Seeder technology for wheat after rice

3.3 Efficient Water Management
- Drip and sprinkler irrigation
- Alternate Wetting and Drying (AWD) in rice: Saves 30% water
- Rainwater harvesting
- Watershed management

3.4 Integrated Nutrient Management
- Balanced fertilization based on soil test
- Organic manures to reduce chemical fertilizer use
- Slow-release fertilizers
- Neem-coated urea to reduce N2O emissions

3.5 Agroforestry
- Trees on farmland provide shade, fodder, timber
- Carbon sequestration
- Reduces temperature extremes
- Improves biodiversity

4. GREENHOUSE GAS MITIGATION

4.1 Rice Cultivation
- Alternate Wetting and Drying (AWD) reduces methane by 30–50%
- Aerobic rice cultivation
- Avoid burning of rice straw

4.2 Livestock
- Improved feed quality reduces methane from enteric fermentation
- Biogas from manure

4.3 Soil Carbon Sequestration
- Conservation tillage
- Cover crops
- Organic matter addition

5. CLIMATE INFORMATION SERVICES
- Agro-meteorological advisory services
- Seasonal weather forecasts
- Crop-specific advisories
- Mobile-based weather alerts (Kisan SMS)

6. GOVERNMENT INITIATIVES
- National Action Plan on Climate Change (NAPCC)
- National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)
- Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) for climate risk
- ICAR-National Innovations in Climate Resilient Agriculture (NICRA)

7. CARBON CREDITS
- Farmers can earn carbon credits through CSA practices
- Voluntary carbon markets
- REDD+ for forest conservation
