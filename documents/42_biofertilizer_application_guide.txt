BIOFERTILIZER APPLICATION GUIDE
================================

1. INTRODUCTION
Biofertilizers are preparations containing living microorganisms that, when applied to seeds, plant surfaces, or soil, colonize the rhizosphere and promote plant growth by increasing nutrient availability. They are eco-friendly alternatives to chemical fertilizers.

2. TYPES OF BIOFERTILIZERS

2.1 Nitrogen-Fixing Biofertilizers
- Rhizobium: Symbiotic N-fixation in legumes (50–200 kg N/ha)
- Azotobacter: Free-living N-fixation in non-legumes (20–30 kg N/ha)
- Azospirillum: Associative N-fixation in cereals (20–40 kg N/ha)
- Blue-Green Algae (BGA): N-fixation in flooded rice (20–30 kg N/ha)
- Azolla: Aquatic fern with N-fixing cyanobacteria (40–60 kg N/ha)

2.2 Phosphate Solubilizing Biofertilizers
- Bacillus megaterium: Solubilizes fixed phosphorus
- Pseudomonas striata: Produces organic acids
- Aspergillus awamori: Fungal PSB
- Penicillium bilaji: Effective in alkaline soils

2.3 Potassium Mobilizing Biofertilizers
- Frateuria aurantia: Mobilizes fixed potassium
- Bacillus mucilaginosus: Silicate bacteria

2.4 Mycorrhizal Biofertilizers
- VAM (Vesicular Arbuscular Mycorrhiza): Improves P, Zn, water uptake
- Glomus intraradices, G. mosseae: Common species
- Increases root surface area 10–100 times

3. APPLICATION METHODS

3.1 Seed Treatment
- Mix biofertilizer with jaggery solution (10%)
- Coat seeds uniformly
- Dry in shade for 30 minutes
- Sow immediately
- Dose: 200 g biofertilizer per 10 kg seed

3.2 Seedling Root Dip
- Prepare slurry of biofertilizer in water
- Dip seedling roots for 30 minutes
- Transplant immediately
- Dose: 1 kg biofertilizer in 10 L water

3.3 Soil Application
- Mix biofertilizer with compost or FYM
- Apply in furrows at sowing
- Dose: 5 kg biofertilizer + 50 kg compost per hectare

3.4 Fertigation
- Mix in irrigation water
- Apply through drip system
- Dose: 5 L liquid biofertilizer per hectare

4. CROP-WISE RECOMMENDATIONS

| Crop      | Biofertilizer          | Dose        |
|-----------|------------------------|-------------|
| Rice      | Azospirillum + PSB     | 200 g/10 kg |
| Wheat     | Azotobacter + PSB      | 200 g/10 kg |
| Maize     | Azospirillum + PSB     | 200 g/10 kg |
| Soybean   | Rhizobium + PSB        | 200 g/10 kg |
| Groundnut | Rhizobium + PSB        | 200 g/10 kg |
| Sugarcane | Azotobacter + PSB      | 5 kg/ha     |

5. PRECAUTIONS
- Do not mix with chemical fertilizers or pesticides
- Store at 4–10°C away from direct sunlight
- Use before expiry date
- Check for contamination before use
- Apply in evening to avoid UV damage

6. BENEFITS
- Reduces chemical fertilizer use by 20–25%
- Improves soil health and microbial activity
- Cost-effective (Rs. 50–100 per kg)
- Environmentally friendly
- Improves crop yield by 10–20%
