WHEAT CULTIVATION GUIDE
=======================

1. INTRODUCTION
Wheat (Triticum aestivum) is the most widely grown cereal crop in the world and a primary source of carbohydrates and protein. In India, wheat is the second most important food grain after rice, grown mainly in the Indo-Gangetic plains.

2. CLIMATE REQUIREMENTS
- Temperature: 10–25°C during growing season; cool winters preferred
- Rainfall: 750–1000 mm annually
- Frost-free period of at least 100 days required
- Hot dry winds during grain filling reduce yield

3. SOIL REQUIREMENTS
- Soil type: Well-drained loam to clay loam soils
- pH range: 6.0–7.5
- Deep fertile soils with good organic matter
- Avoid waterlogged or saline soils

4. VARIETIES
- HD-2967, HD-3086 (North India, high yielding)
- GW-322, GW-496 (Gujarat, drought tolerant)
- K-307, K-9107 (Uttar Pradesh)
- Durum wheat: HI-8498, PDW-233 (for pasta)

5. LAND PREPARATION
- Deep ploughing after kharif harvest
- 2–3 harrowing to achieve fine tilth
- Apply 10 tonnes/ha FYM before last ploughing
- Ensure proper drainage channels

6. SOWING
- Sowing time: October–November (Rabi season)
- Seed rate: 100–125 kg/ha
- Row spacing: 22.5 cm
- Sowing depth: 5–6 cm
- Seed treatment with Thiram 2.5 g/kg seed

7. IRRIGATION
- First irrigation (Crown Root Initiation): 20–25 DAS
- Second irrigation (Tillering): 40–45 DAS
- Third irrigation (Jointing): 60–65 DAS
- Fourth irrigation (Flowering): 80–85 DAS
- Fifth irrigation (Grain filling): 100–105 DAS
- Total water requirement: 350–450 mm

8. FERTILIZER APPLICATION
- Nitrogen: 120 kg/ha (half basal, half at first irrigation)
- Phosphorus: 60 kg/ha (full basal)
- Potassium: 40 kg/ha (full basal)
- Sulphur: 20 kg/ha for quality improvement

9. WEED MANAGEMENT
- Isoproturon 75 WP at 1 kg a.i./ha for narrow-leaf weeds
- Metsulfuron methyl for broad-leaf weeds
- Manual weeding at 30–35 DAS

10. HARVESTING
- Harvest when grain moisture is 12–14%
- Crop matures in 110–130 days
- Use combine harvester for large fields
- Average yield: 4–5 tonnes/ha irrigated

11. STORAGE
- Clean and dry grain to 12% moisture
- Store in gunny bags or silos
- Fumigate with Aluminium Phosphide tablets
